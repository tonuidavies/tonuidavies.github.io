Uniting Girls to Change the World

Every girl, no matter where she is born, deserves to dream. You can make her dreams a reality.

We’re the leading global community of passionate advocates changing policies and raising funds to support United Nations programs that help the hardest to reach girls living in places where it is hardest to be a girl.
